Internships

• Persistent Martian Program

  • Intern |

    • June 2023 to Ongoing

During my internship in the Persistent Martian Program, I gained hands-on experience and practical knowledge in various technologies, including Data Structures and Algorithms, Computer Networks, Java Programming Language, Operating Systems, and Database Management Systems (DBMS).

• PERSISTENT / THE SMARTBRIDGE

  • Salesforce Developer Intern |

    • Oct 2022 to Jan 2023

Developed a robust Salesforce-based application utilizing Apex, Aura Framework, SOQL, and SOSL to effectively display, analyze, and visualize the status of ongoing projects within an organization. The application offers advanced features and functionalities to streamline project management and provide valuable insights. By leveraging Apex, Aura Framework, SOQL, and SOSL, the enhanced application empowers organizations to gain comprehensive visibility into project statuses, make informed decisions, and drive successful project outcomes. The seamless integration, advanced analytics, and enhanced security features make it a valuable tool for project management and performance analysis.

• EI SYSTEMS TECHNOLOGIES

  • Web Development Intern |

    • June 2022 to Aug 2022

During my involvement in the development of ZODION, I primarily focused on the front-end and basic user interface (UI) aspects of the website. ZODION is a site that showcases the annual traits and characteristics of each zodiac sign, providing users with insights into astrology. To bring ZODION to life, I utilized a combination of HTML, CSS, JavaScript, and the fundamental libraries of React. These technologies allowed me to create a visually appealing and interactive user interface that seamlessly presents the information about each zodiac sign.

• BHARAT HEAVY ELECTRICALS LIMITED

  • Research and Development Intern |

    • May 2022 to June 2022

During my involvement in the improvement of hydroelectric power plant operations, I focused on enhancing the efficiency of impoundment, diversion, and pumping methods. As part of this project, I successfully implemented a regeneration prototype that incorporates both impoundment and pumping techniques, ultimately increasing the overall efficiency of the power plant.
